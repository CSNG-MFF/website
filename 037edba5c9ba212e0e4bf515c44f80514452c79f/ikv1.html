NAIL087: Informatics and Cognitive Science

Synopsis

This course gives an introduction into computational neuroscience and cognitive psychology. The first semester focuses on basics of neural computation, including canonical models of neurons, architecture of cortical circuitry and its function and information processing in neural substrate. This basic knowledge is then built upon to introduce students to principles of cognition in several example systems, including vision and motor system.

Requirements

1) A computational modelling task
2) A psychological experiment
3) Participation in a psychological experiment
4) Written test: Required reading: Bear, ch. 1 - 5
