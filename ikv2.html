NAIL088: Informatics and Cognitive Science 2

Synopsis

This course gives an introduction into computational neuroscience and cognitive psychology. The second term will focus primarily on visual system, computational modelling, memory systems, spatial cognition, motor systems, but neural and behavioral learning will also be covered. This course runs in English.

Lecture overview and slides

The course will take place every Friday at 10:40-13:00 in S3. The first 90 minutes will be lecture followed by 45 minute excercise session.
